 
This LCD has the following pinout:

VCC (5V)
GND
WR
DAT
CS

And after trial and error, this happens to be a (a clone of?) the
HT162x lcd controller series.

There are 16 7 segments digits plus some miscellaneous icons right below.

No accurate mapping has been discovered yet tough
(setting a bit at a time could be an effective way to find a pattern).
